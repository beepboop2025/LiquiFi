## FINAL VERDICT

### Can you reach Series A solo with AI?

**Traditional Series A (₹25-40 Cr, ₹100 Cr valuation):**
❌ **No.** Physically impossible. Requires team, SOC 2 auditor, 50 enterprise customers.

**"Micro-Series A" (₹10-15 Cr, ₹50 Cr valuation):**
⚠️ **Maybe.** 20% chance. Requires:
- Exceptional execution
- 80+ hour weeks for 18 months
- ₹15-20L MRR from 15-20 customers
- Acceptance of higher risk (no formal SLA)

**Bootstrapped to ₹10L MRR:**
✅ **Yes.** Most realistic. Skip VC, keep full ownership.

### My Recommendation

**Don't chase Series A solo.** Instead:

1. **Months 1-6:** Build with AI, aim for ₹2-3L MRR
2. **Month 7:** Raise tiny seed (₹1-2 Cr) from angels
3. **Months 8-12:** Hire 2-3 people (engineer + sales)
4. **Month 18:** Now you're ready for real Series A

**Total time:** 18 months (not 6)
**Team at Series A:** 4-5 people (not 25)
**Raise at Series A:** ₹15-20 Cr (not ₹35-40 Cr)

This is achievable. Pure solo Series A is not.
