> "Namaste [Name] ji, I'm [Your Name], a Jaipur-based fintech founder. We've built a treasury automation tool specifically for Rajasthan NBFCs/manufacturers to optimize cash yields. Can I show you a 5-minute demo?"

- "Jaipur-based" = local credibility
- "Rajasthan NBFCs" = shows you know their market
- "5-minute" = low commitment

1. **For NBFCs:**
   - "Gold loan liquidity buffers sitting idle?"

   - "Raw material payments timing affecting working capital?"
   - "Export receivables FX exposure?"
   - "Seasonal cash flow management?"

3. **For Real Estate:**
   - "Construction milestone payments timing?"
   - "Customer EMIs vs vendor payments mismatch?"
   - "Parking funds in FDs vs better options?"
